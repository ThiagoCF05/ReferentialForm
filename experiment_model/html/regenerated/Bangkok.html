Bangkok

Bangkok , known in Thai as Krung Thep (กรุงเทพฯ), is the capital and largest city of Thailand, with an official 2000 census population of 6,355,144. It is located at , on the east bank of the Chao Phraya River, near the Gulf of Thailand.

Krung Thep has been the proper name of Bangkok for more than two centuries. However, its original name of Bangkok (which now is used locally to refer only to a district of it ) persists in being used by most foreigners. (See History and Full Name below.)

Bangkok is a fast growing, economically and culturally dynamic city in Southeast Asia. The World Meteorological Organization has dubbed it the world's hottest large city. Moreover, Bangkok is one of the world's most popular tourist destinations. It is the wealthiest and most populated city in Thailand. Bangkok is ranked as the 21st most populated city in the world
