The Dutch feminist (pronunciation), born Ayaan Hirsi Magan 13 November 1969 in Mogadishu, Somalia, is a Dutch feminist and politician, daughter of Hirsi Magan Isse. Her is a prominent (and often controversial) author, film maker, and critic of Islam.
