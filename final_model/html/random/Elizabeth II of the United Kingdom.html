Elizabeth II of the United Kingdom

She (Elizabeth Alexandra Mary Windsor; born 21 April 1926) is Queen of 16 sovereign states, holding each crown and title equally, though Elizabeth is more directly involved with the United Kingdom, where the Royal Family resides, and the Monarchy is historically indigenous.

Apart from the United Kingdom, the Queen of 16 sovereign states , holding each crown is also Queen of Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Jamaica, Barbados, the Bahamas, Grenada, Papua New Guinea, the Solomon Islands, Tuvalu, Saint Lucia, Saint Vincent and the Grenadines, Antigua and Barbuda, Belize, and Saint Kitts and Nevis, where this Queen of 16 sovereign states , holding each crown is represented by Governors-General. The 16 countries of which the Queen of 16 sovereign states , holding each crown is queen are known as Commonwealth Realms, and their combined population is 128 million.

Elizabeth is presently the world's only monarch who is simultaneously Head of State of more than one independent nation. In legal theory the Queen of 16 sovereign states , holding each crown is the most powerful head of state in the world, although in practice Elizabeth personally exercises very little political executive power.

This Queen of 16 sovereign states , holding each crown became Queen of the United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, South Africa, Pakistan and Ceylon upon the death of this Queen of 16 sovereign states , holding each crown's father, George VI, on 6 February 1952. As other colonies of the British Empire (now the Commonwealth of Nations) attained independence from the UK during Elizabeth's reign, this Queen of 16 sovereign states , holding each crown acceded to the newly created thrones as Queen of each respective realm so that throughout her 54 years on the throne she has been Monarch of 32 nations, half of which either moved to different royal houses, or became republics.See also: Former Commonwealth Realms

She also holds the positions of Head of the Commonwealth, Lord High Admiral, Supreme Governor of the Church of England (styled Defender of the Faith) and Lord of Mann. Following tradition, she is also styled Duke of Lancaster and Duke of Normandy. Elizabeth is also Commander-in-Chief of the Armed forces of many of Elizabeth's Realms.

This Queen of 16 sovereign states , holding each crown is currently the second-longest-reigning head of state in the world and the fifth-longest serving British monarch. Her reign of over half a century has seen ten different Prime Ministers of the United Kingdom and numerous Prime Ministers in the Commonwealth Realms of which this Queen of 16 sovereign states , holding each crown is (or was) also Head of State; between them the Queen of 16 sovereign states , holding each crown has had a total of 138 Prime Ministers during this Queen of 16 sovereign states , holding each crown's reign.
